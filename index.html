About Us

Discover the Flavorful Story of Cafe Moringa

At Cafe Moringa, we're not just a restaurant; we're a vibrant culinary adventure born from the dreams and passion of Botswana's dynamic youth. Founded in 2010, our journey has been a thrilling blend of innovation and tradition. Located at the heart of Gaborone's Central Business District, inside the bustling Fields Mall, Cafe Moringa has carved its place as a gastronomic oasis for all.

Journey

Cafe Moringa sprouted from the vision of our enterprising founders who aimed to create a dining haven that brings together local and international flavors under one roof. Since our inception, we've remained committed to our culinary craft, serving dishes that delight both the palate and the soul.

Savor the Diversity

We embrace diversity and cater to all classes, making Cafe Moringa a true melting pot of flavors. Our menu is a tapestry of local and international cuisines, designed to tantalize your taste buds and broaden your culinary horizons. From hearty local favorites to exquisite global creations, our offerings are a tribute to Botswana's rich gastronomic heritage.

Our Success

What began as a youthful dream has grown into a flourishing culinary empire. Cafe Moringa has become a household name, beloved by the people of Botswana and cherished by international visitors. Our success is a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and a genuine love for food.

Join Us Today

Come experience Cafe Moringa's exceptional fusion of flavors and our commitment to delivering a delightful dining experience. Whether you're a local food enthusiast or a traveler seeking a taste of Botswana's vibrant culture, we're here to welcome you with open arms and a table full of delectable dishes.


Thank you for being a part of our journey. We can't wait to serve you and create lasting memories together.
